About This Site

What do you think of this site? Why do you think I made it the way it is?

Who is the intended user?

It's me. I'm the intended user. And thus, the site is given purpose. I needed a directory that I can easily access on the internet from any device and have access to select files and servers. There are many ways to do this, I have chosen a website for the time being. This site has no fancy styling or graphics. It is a purely functional site written in HTML and CSS. The only piece of JavaScript is a one-liner in the contact section that obfuscates my email so that I reduce the chance of receiving spam mail.
